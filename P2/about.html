Meet the Faces Behind

Drawfee

The One's Responsible for Bringing your Dumb Ideas to Life!

Former College Humor sub-series, now completely independent; is live recorded drawing session series on YouTube, where the artists make drawings based off taken suggestions. Which is prompted by their intro: “Where we take your dumb ideas, and make even dumber drawings”, which then they will give unscripted discussion revolving around the drawing, often giving very unhinged comments, which they apologize for at the end of every video.

I'm Nathan!

Nathan Yaffe is a former writer /illustrator for College Humor. Yaffe co-started the Drawfee Show with Caldwell Tanner(who stepped down as Co-Host.) He now hosts the show with Julia Leptit.

I'm Jacob!

Jacob Andrews worked as a freelance comic artist. He then started to work as an Illustrator at College Humor, working his way up to Head Illustrator before working Full-time as a Drawfee Show. Andrews also works as a content stratagist.

I'm Karina!

Karina Farek was a Buzzfeed hire for illustration, but soon became a freelance artist that often work for Buzzfeed and College Humor. Farek first appear as a Drawfee guest artist before becoming a full time host.

And I'm Julia!

Julia Lepetit interened then worked at Dorkly as Head Illustrator and Managing Editor. Lepetit appeared as one of the very first guest at the Drawfee show before becoming a full-time host and leaving Dorlky.
